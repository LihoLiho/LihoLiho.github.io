WHAT IS AMOK?

Rock grunge / Future grunge / Post rock

Created during summer 2016 in the Waremmian region on the remains of Acid Test and the Blights.

Amok has metamorphosed during the years into its actual formation: A trio eager to display its wildness.
Let their bittersweet melodies mesmerize you. You will be facing a sometimes dreamy, other times ravaving wall of sound.

On the brink of explosion, chaos is tamed and controlled. So, take a seat and get ready. We are taking off to an unknown location.
